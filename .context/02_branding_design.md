# Eldoraigne Tennis Club - Branding & Design System

## Core Brand Colors
- **Brand Blue (Primary):** `#0B3173` (Main headers, sidebar, primary buttons)
- **Brand Gold (Accent):** `#D4AF37` (Highlights, star items, user-specific badges)
- **Brand Red (Alert/Goal):** `#EF4444` (Shortfalls, urgent maintenance, delete actions)
- **Brand Green (Success):** `#10B981` (Processed claims, completed tasks)
- **Background Grey:** `#F1F5F9` (Global app background)

## UI Components & Visual Language
- **Panels (Glassmorphism):** Use `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.90)` with a backdrop-filter blur of `12px`.
- **Shadows (Premium):** `0 10px 30px -5px rgba(11, 49, 115, 0.15)`
- **Border Radius:** `1rem` (16px) for cards and buttons (`rounded-xl` in Tailwind).
- **Typography:** Use 'Inter' font. Headings should be bold with tight tracking.

## Gradients
- **Blue Gradient:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #0B3173 0%, #062356 100%)`
- **Gold Gradient:** `linear-gradient(135deg, #D4AF37 0%, #F3E5AB 100%)`
